Common Investment Questions Guide

Get daily insights about the market and 24/7 support from our experts and make smart trading decisions.

How does investment work?

Investment is a process in which you invest your money in the financial markets such as forex, stocks or commodities and this money is earned back according to the market dynamics and trading patterns.

Is my capital secure?

The security of your capital lies with the market conditions and risk management practices. Although powerful strategies are meant to safeguard funds, there is no risk-free investment.

What risks should expect?

The market risks which you should anticipate to affect your investment performance include price volatility, economic changes and the occurrence of unforeseen global events.

How returns are calculated?

The returns are computed by comparing the performance of the trades or investments over time normally on a percentage gain or loss on your capital invested.

When can withdraw funds?

The policies of withdrawal are based on the terms of the platform. In most cases, withdrawal of funds may be done after a certain time or after certain conditions are fulfilled.

Who manages investments here?

Experienced traders or financial experts are the ones who normally handle investments, and make informed decisions based on market analysis and strategies.
