Do I need trading experience to use automated social trading?

There is no previous experience. Automated social trading at Market Investopedia is aimed at enabling novices to engage in the process as they learn how strategies and disciplined trading choices operate.

What is the minimum account size for automated trading?

The minimum size of the account varies with the broker and strategy. Market Investopedia advises traders on the right amount of capital to start with and the right amount of risk to allocate before automated systems are used.

Can automated trading guarantee profits?

No trading system can be automated to assure profits. Market Investopedia focuses on realistic expectations, adequate risk management, and knowledge of market conditions in the use of automation tools.

How are risks managed in automated trading systems?

Risk is addressed using predefined rules like position sizing, stop losses and trade filters. Market Investopedia incorporates these controls to facilitate disciplined implementation.

Yes, in most financial markets, trading bots are legal when they are used with regulated brokers. Compliance and responsible use of trading technology is also described by Market Investopedia.
